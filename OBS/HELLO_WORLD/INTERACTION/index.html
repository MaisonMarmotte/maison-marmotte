Hello there, this page exists to give contect to something you might have received elsewhere.
you are not meant to find everything here.
If you received a physical card, it contains the full promt.
There is no obligation to take part
There is no correct way to respond
This project comes from a sincere intrest in how identity id expressed, constructed and shared.

For context this quest primarily exsists online
it moves from hand to hand
This page is here to provide context and archive a section of responses of people who chose to share them.

If you have already received a card, you should already have acces to the full promt.
responses may be submitted trough any medium, writing drawing , references or other.
Passing the quest from person to person is part of the process, but participation is always optional.

If you do not yet have a card with the promt and you wish to participate you may request one by emailing to contact@maisonmarmotte.be
Any work you chose to share will only be publihed with your explicit consent and you may choose to remain anonymous

Data usage and privacy

Submissions are never sold, shard with third parties or used for profiling
They may be used as inspiration for future work within Maison Marmotte. You are free to withdraw your submission and we will delete your data.
