MM / OBS-01 / SURVEY

>>INTRO

This page exists to give context to an offline project.

If a card reached you, you were invited into a small creative exercise. It moves from hand to hand.

This project begins from a sincere interest in how people express identity, and what they choose to wear, share, or hold back.

>>HOW TO PARTICIPATE

If you received a card, you already have access to the full prompt.

You may respond through writing, drawing, references, or any other form.

Passing the quest from person to person is part of the process, but participation is always optional.

If you do not have a card and wish to participate, you may request one by emailing contact@maisonmarmotte.be.

>>DATA USAGE / PRIVACY

Submissions are never sold, shared with third parties, or used for profiling.

Any work you choose to share will only be published with your explicit consent. You may remain anonymous.

You are free to withdraw your submission at any time.
