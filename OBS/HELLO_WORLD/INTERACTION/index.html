INTRO

Hello there, this page exists to give context to something you might have received elsewhere.

You are not meant to find everything here.
If you received a physical card, it contains the full promt.
There is no obligation to take part.
There is no correct way to respond.
This project comes from a sincere intrest in how identity is expressed, constructed and shared.

HOW TO PARTICIPATE

If you have already received a card, you should already have acces to the full promt.
responses may be submitted trough any medium, writing drawing , references or other.
Passing the quest from person to person is part of the process, but participation is always optional.

If you do not have a card and wish to participate, you may request one by emailing contact@maisonmarmotte.be.

Any work you chose to share will only be publihed with your explicit consent and you may choose to remain anonymous

DATA USAGE / PRIVACY

Submissions are never sold, shared with third parties, or used for profiling.

Any work you choose to share will only be published with your explicit consent. You may remain anonymous.

You are free to withdraw your submission at any time, we will delete your data.
