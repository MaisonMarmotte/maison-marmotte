HELLO WORLD

It is the first day of school after summer break.
You wake up and get ready to step back into the world.
The day feels new: exciting, unfamiliar, a little overwhelming. You may be meeting new people. You want to wear something comfortable enough to move through the day, but also something that feels protective — almost like a small form of armor. Something that helps you feel held, present, and true to yourself.

Now imagine you could snap the perfect outfit out of thin air......

1 /What would it look like?

What pieces would it contain? Tops, bottoms, layers, maybe a dress, a coat? What would your bag look like, will you wear a bag and accessories? Describe the garments, the silhouette, the textures, the colors, or simply the feeling of it.

2 /If words are not enough...

You can also answer through imagery, a poem, a song, a film or a character.

3 /Why this outfit?

What does it do for you? What does it protect, communicate or express?

4 /What matters most to you when choosing
what to wear on a day like this
